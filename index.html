Academic profile

Language technology with global reach and local purpose.

Arbi Haza Nasution is a computational linguistics and NLP scholar focused on LLMs, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, bilingual lexicon induction, multilingual machine translation, and Indonesian ethnic languages. His contributions to inclusive language technology led to invitations to UNESCO’s Language Technologies for All conferences in Paris in 2019 and 2025.
